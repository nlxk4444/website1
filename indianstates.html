Welcome to Indian States

India is a diverse country with 28 states and 8 union territories. Each state has its own unique culture, history, and geography.

North India

Punjab

Punjab is known for its rich agricultural land, vibrant culture, and historical significance.

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a mountainous state known for its natural beauty, trekking trails, and adventure sports.

South India

Kerala

Kerala is known for its backwaters, beaches, and rich cultural heritage.

Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is known for its ancient temples, vibrant culture, and rich history.

East India

West Bengal

West Bengal is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and vibrant city life.

Odisha

Odisha is known for its ancient temples, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage.

West India

Maharashtra

Maharashtra is known for its vibrant city life, rich cultural heritage, and historical significance.

Gujarat

Gujarat is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and vibrant city life.
